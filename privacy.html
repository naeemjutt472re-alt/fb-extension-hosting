Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how our app/extension handles information when you use Facebook Login or connect your Facebook Page with the extension.

Information We Collect

- Facebook Page Access Token (to fetch conversations and send replies).
- Page-related conversation data (messages, participants) temporarily in your browser storage.

How We Use Information

- To allow you to view and manage conversations from your Facebook Page.
- To send messages you choose to send to your page subscribers.
- We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser extension storage. We do not store user data on external servers.

Data Deletion

If you want to delete your data, you can remove the app from Facebook settings, or directly email us at faisalboss788@gmail.com. See our Data Deletion Instructions page.
