Data Deletion Instructions

This app/extension does not store personal data on its own servers. If you want to request deletion of your Facebook-related data connected through this app, please follow either option below:

Option A — Remove the App from Facebook

  1. Open your Facebook account settings.
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy → Settings → Business Integrations / Apps and Websites.
  3. Find this app and remove it.

Once removed, Facebook will revoke tokens and disconnect the app. We do not retain data afterward.

Option B — Contact Us Directly

Email: faisalboss788@gmail.com
Include your Facebook Page ID or the email/username associated with your account so we can verify and process the deletion request.

Developer Callback (for Facebook Verification)

If Facebook sends a signed_request for user data deletion, we respond per Meta policy with a confirmation code and status URL.
